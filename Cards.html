Cards

First Drafts

Circles and dots formed a super-high-speed track, together with the shape of the world, means C-MIST is super fast and world connected.
Diamond is used to refer to ‘value’, and those stars mean ‘value’ from the whole universe.


Second Drafts - Front Side

The material is the same semi-transparent material with coins, which keeps the consistency and makes it more solid.
The same energy wave is also used as a security texture.


Second Drafts - Backside

Due to the semitransparency of the material, the backside looks just the same with the front side, but the opacify of the information is lower. So that when those two cards connect, ID information can be shown and seen clearly on the backside.


ID Information - Backside

Both of C-MIST card and Bankcard can be used to prove the identity. But when those two cards magnetised together, card holder’s information will be shown on the backside, just as our current ID card.
Also, due to the year of 2146 is not a stable and peaceful time, and might have some potential species appeared, the ID information will also include Race and Criminal Record.


Finalised Bankcard

It’s dynamic on the surface.
Also, whenever there is a transaction, the sum of transaction will flow across the surface of the card, followed by the balance.

Finalised C-MIST Card

Whenever passengers used C-MIST transportation services, from the card surface, passengers can see where they are in between one station to another.
